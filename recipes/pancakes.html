Pancake

This is an image depicting our final result.

This recipe is about making really good looking pancakes!

It includes eggs, milk, and flower so be aware of these allergies when cooking for guests.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cup of all-pourpose flower
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Steps:

  1. Mix the eggs, milk, and melted butter in a large bowl.
  2. Gradually add the baking powder, flower, and sugar.
  3. lightly oil pan or skillet.
  4. pour them onto a pan or skillet.
  5. Cook in a skillet until golden brown each side.
