French Toast

This is an image depicting our final result.

This recipe is about making really good looking french toast!

It includes eggs, milk, and cinnamon so be aware of these allergies when cooking for guests.

Ingredients:

  • 6 Thick Slices of Bread
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2/3 Cup Milk
  • 1 teaspoon Vinilla Extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon Cinnimon
  • 1 tablespoon Butter

Steps:

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, and vinilla together in a shallow bowl.
  2. Gradually add butter and cinnamon while mixing.
  3. Dunk the bread in the newly made batter.
  4. Cook in a skillet until golden brown.
